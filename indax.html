Welcome to Pillory

Between life and death is a bridge - above it lies holy Promontory. Below it, "The Cavity". The guardians of Promontory were tasked to maintain it, but have since become complacent.

You are a lost creature, trapped at the bottom of it.

On your journey, you will meet strange friends, and even stranger foes. Some are like you - lost, scrambling to understand why they exist. Some are complacent, careless in their afterlife.

Some might want to hurt you.
